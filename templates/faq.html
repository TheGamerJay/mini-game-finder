❓ Mini Game Finder — FAQ

General

Q: What is Mini Game Finder?

A: Mini Game Finder is a fun, family-friendly arcade site focused on word puzzles. It's designed to encourage learning, creativity, and friendly competition.

Q: Who can play?

A: Mini Game Finder is suitable for players of all ages. Younger players should have guidance from a parent or guardian.

Accounts & Access

Q: Do I need an account to play?

A: ✅ Yes. An account is required to play Mini Game Finder. This ensures that your progress, scores, and credits are saved securely.

Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?

A: Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password.

Q: Can I create more than one account?

A: No. Each person should only have one account. Duplicate accounts may be restricted or removed to keep gameplay fair.

Credits & Play

Q: What are credits?

A: Credits are virtual points used to enjoy games and features inside Mini Game Finder. They are just for fun and have no real-world money value.

Q: Do credits expire?

A: No. Credits linked to your account remain active as long as your account is in good standing.

Q: Can I buy or sell credits?

A: No. Credits cannot be sold, exchanged, or withdrawn outside of Mini Game Finder.

Community & Safety

Q: What is the Community page?

A: The Community page is a friendly space to celebrate wins. Players can post about their achievements, and others can react with positive responses such as likes or hearts.

Q: Can players chat with each other?

A: No. To keep things safe and family-friendly, there is no private chat or messaging.

Q: What happens if someone posts something inappropriate?

A: Our team monitors the community to keep it positive and welcoming. Posts that break the rules may be removed.

Policies & Support

Q: Where are the official rules and policies?

A: You can review our Terms of Service and our Privacy Policy.

Q: Is my information safe?

A: Yes. Mini Game Finder is designed to be secure and family-friendly. Your personal information is protected and never sold.

Q: What if I need help?

A: Email our support team at soulbridgeai.contact@gmail.com for assistance.

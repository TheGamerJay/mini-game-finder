Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

At Mini Game Finder, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details (such as username, email, and password) when you sign up.
  • Gameplay data (scores, progress, achievements) to enhance your experience.
  • Technical information (browser type, device, IP address) for security and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Mini Game Finder account.
  • To save your progress and provide gameplay features.
  • To improve site performance and fix technical issues.
  • To keep the community safe and family-friendly.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. Data may be shared only:

  • When required by law or legal process.
  • To protect the rights and safety of Mini Game Finder and its users.
  • With trusted service providers who help us operate the site (e.g., hosting, security).

4. Cookies

Mini Game Finder uses cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work properly.

5. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee complete security.

6. Children's Privacy

Mini Game Finder is family-friendly. Players under 13 should use the site only with a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without parental consent.

7. Your Choices

  • You can update your account details anytime in your profile.
  • You may request account deletion by contacting us through our Support page.

8. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the site means you accept the latest version.

9. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our Support page.
